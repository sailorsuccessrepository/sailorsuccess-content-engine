# STCW Courses Complete Guide 2026

STCW (Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping) courses are mandatory international maritime training requirements. Without these certificates, you cannot work on any commercial vessel.

## What is STCW?

**STCW = Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping for Seafarers**

- International IMO convention
- Sets minimum training standards
- Recognized by all flag states
- Mandatory for all seafarers
- Regular updates (Manila Amendments)

## STCW Basic Safety Courses

### The "Basic Four" (Required for Everyone)

| Course | Full Name | Duration |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| PST | Personal Survival Techniques | 2 days |
| PSSR | Personal Safety & Social Responsibilities | 2 days |
| EFA | Elementary First Aid | 2 days |
| FPFF | Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting | 3 days |

### PST (Personal Survival Techniques)

**Covers**:
- Survival at sea principles
- Life-saving appliances
- Lifeboat and life raft operations
- Survival craft equipment
- Helicopter rescue techniques

**Practical Training**:
- Swimming with lifejacket
- Life raft boarding
- Survival techniques in water
- Distress signal usage

### PSSR (Personal Safety & Social Responsibilities)

**Covers**:
- Shipboard safety
- Accident prevention
- Communication onboard
- Human relations
- Fatigue awareness
- Environmental protection

**Key Learning**:
- Understanding shipboard organization
- Safety protocols
- Emergency procedures
- Social responsibilities

### EFA (Elementary First Aid)

**Covers**:
- Basic first aid techniques
- CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)
- Wound treatment
- Fracture handling
- Shock management
- Emergency response

**Practical Training**:
- CPR practice
- Bandaging techniques
- Patient handling
- Emergency scenarios

### FPFF (Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting)

**Covers**:
- Fire chemistry and types
- Fire prevention methods
- Fire-fighting equipment
- Fixed installations
- Fire emergency procedures

**Practical Training**:
- Portable extinguisher use
- Hose handling
- SCBA (breathing apparatus)
- Live fire exercises

## Course Details - Advanced

### GOC (GMDSS)

**Full Name**: General Operator's Certificate
**Duration**: 6-8 weeks
**Purpose**: Radio communication certification
**Required For**: All deck officers
**Includes**: Practical radio operation

### ECDIS

**Full Name**: Electronic Chart Display and Information System
**Duration**: 5 days (generic)
**Purpose**: Electronic navigation certification
**Additional**: Type-specific training required
**Required For**: All deck officers on ECDIS-equipped vessels

### Medical Care (Medical First Aid)

**Duration**: 5 days
**Purpose**: Advanced first aid for officers
**Required For**: Officers designated for medical duties
**Covers**: Detailed medical procedures onboard

### Ship Security Officer (SSO)

**Duration**: 3 days
**Purpose**: ISPS Code implementation
**Required For**: Designated security officers
**Covers**: Ship security assessment and plans

## STCW Certificate Validity

### Standard Validity Periods

| Course Category | Validity |
|-----------------|----------|
| Basic Safety (PST, EFA, FPFF, PSSR) | 5 years |
| Proficiency in Survival Craft | 5 years |
| Advanced Fire Fighting | 5 years |
| GMDSS/GOC | No expiry (endorsement: 5 years) |
| ECDIS Generic | No expiry |
| Medical Care | 5 years |

### Revalidation Requirements

**Options for Revalidation**:
1. Approved refresher course, OR
2. Approved sea service in past 5 years + assessment

**Refresher Course Duration**: Usually 1-2 days

## FAQs

### Can I do all basic safety courses together?

Yes, most institutes offer combined packages for all four basic courses. Total duration is about 10 days.

### Are STCW certificates valid worldwide?

Yes, STCW is an international standard. Certificates from approved institutes are recognized globally.

### What happens if my STCW expires while onboard?

You may continue current voyage but must revalidate before next contract. Plan renewals in advance.

### Do I need tanker courses if joining bulk carriers?

No, tanker courses are specific to tanker vessels. But having them opens more job options.

### Can I do STCW courses online?

Practical components require physical attendance. Some theory may be online, but certification requires physical training.
