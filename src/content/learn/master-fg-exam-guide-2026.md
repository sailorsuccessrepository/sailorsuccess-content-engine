# Master (FG) Examination 2026: Complete Guide

The Master (FG) or Master Foreign Going examination is the pinnacle of deck officer certifications. Passing this examination along with command assessment qualifies you to take command of any merchant vessel as Captain/Master anywhere in the world.

## Examination Overview

### Conducting Authority
- Directorate General of Shipping, Mumbai
- Under the Merchant Shipping Act, 1958

### Eligibility Criteria
1. Hold Mate (FG) Certificate of Competency
2. Minimum 36 months sea service as OOW (Officer of Watch)
3. Of which, 12 months as Chief Officer
4. All STCW certificates valid
5. Medical fitness certificate

### Examination Centers
- Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata
- Noida, Goa, Kochi

## Oral Examination

### Format
- Conducted by Senior Surveyor/Chief Examiner
- Duration: 90-120 minutes
- Comprehensive assessment of knowledge

### Topics Covered

**Navigation and Seamanship:**
- COLREGS application in all scenarios
- Traffic Separation Schemes
- Navigation in restricted visibility
- Emergency situations handling

**Ship Handling:**
- Detailed berthing/unberthing plans
- Anchoring in various conditions
- Heavy weather operations
- Search and rescue coordination

**Commercial Aspects:**
- Charter party interpretation
- Bill of lading issues
- Cargo claims handling
- Owner/charterer disputes

**Leadership and Management:**
- Crew management scenarios
- Emergency response leadership
- Port State inspection handling
- ISM Code implementation

**Legal Responsibilities:**
- Master's overriding authority
- Drug and alcohol policies
- Environmental compliance
- Accident investigation

### Common Oral Questions

1. **COLREGS Scenarios:**
   - "You're overtaking vessel in TSS. Actions?"
   - "Vessel NUC ahead showing lights. Your action?"

2. **Ship Handling:**
   - "Describe berthing port side to, wind 30 knots off berth"
   - "Anchor dragging in crowded anchorage. Actions?"

3. **Commercial:**
   - "Charterer orders you to sail with insufficient bunkers"
   - "Cargo damage discovered during discharge"

4. **Emergency:**
   - "Fire in accommodation - comprehensive actions"
   - "Grounding at night in unknown waters"

5. **Legal:**
   - "Master's right to deviate from voyage?"
   - "Drug found onboard - your procedures?"

## Command Assessment

Many companies require command assessment in addition to Master (FG) certificate:

### Assessment Components
1. **Technical Assessment** - Written test by company
2. **Simulator Assessment** - Ship handling evaluation
3. **Interview** - Senior management panel
4. **Sea Assessment** - Supervised voyage as Master

### Preparation for Command Assessment
- Study company-specific procedures
- Review previous Masters' experiences
- Practice simulator exercises
- Develop commercial awareness

## After Passing Master (FG)

### Certificate Issuance
1. Apply to DG Shipping
2. Submit all documents
3. Pay prescribed fees
4. Receive Master (FG) certificate

### First Command Preparation
1. Complete company's command assessment
2. Study vessel-specific documentation
3. Review commercial aspects
4. Mental preparation for responsibility

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long to prepare for Master FG?
Typically 4-6 months of dedicated study, ideally while on leave.

### Can I sail as Master immediately after passing?
No, most companies require command assessment and may assign you as Chief Officer first.

### What's the hardest part of Master FG?
The oral examination is most challenging due to its comprehensive nature and pressure.

### Is Master FG internationally recognized?
Yes, Indian Master FG certificate is recognized worldwide under STCW Convention.
