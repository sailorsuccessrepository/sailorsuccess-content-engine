# STCW Certificates 2026: Complete Guide

STCW (Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping) certificates are mandatory for all seafarers. This guide covers every certificate you need from joining to command.

## What is STCW?

**STCW Convention:**
- International Maritime Organization (IMO) standard
- Minimum training requirements
- Recognized globally
- India is signatory

**Purpose:**
- Ensure safety at sea
- Standardize training worldwide
- Mandatory for all commercial seafarers

## Certificate Categories

### By Career Stage

| Stage | Required Certificates |
|-------|----------------------|
| Pre-Sea | BST/PST, PSSR, EFA, FPFF |
| Junior Officer | + PSCRB |
| Senior Officer | + AFF, MFA |

## Basic Safety Training (BST)

### Components

**1. Personal Survival Techniques (PST)**

| Details | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| Duration | 1-2 days |
| Content | Survival at sea, lifejackets, liferafts |
| Practical | Pool training, liferaft launching |
| Fee | ₹5,000-8,000 |
| Validity | 5 years |

**2. Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting (FPFF)**

| Details | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| Duration | 1.5-2 days |
| Content | Fire types, extinguishers, fire fighting |
| Practical | Live fire fighting |
| Fee | ₹5,000-8,000 |
| Validity | 5 years |

**3. Elementary First Aid (EFA)**

| Details | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| Duration | 1 day |
| Content | Basic first aid, CPR, wound care |
| Practical | Bandaging, CPR practice |
| Fee | ₹3,000-5,000 |
| Validity | 5 years |

**4. Personal Safety & Social Responsibilities (PSSR)**

| Details | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| Duration | 1 day |
| Content | Ship safety, environmental protection |
| Practical | Familiarization exercises |
| Fee | ₹3,000-5,000 |
| Validity | 5 years |

### BST Package

**Combined Course:**
- Duration: 4-5 days
- Fee: ₹15,000-25,000
- All four components
- Recommended for efficiency

## Renewal Process

### When to Renew

**5-Year Certificates:**
- Renew within 5 years
- Don't let them expire
- Plan 3-6 months ahead

### Revalidation vs Full Course

**Revalidation (Refresher):**
- Shorter duration
- Lower cost
- For valid certificate holders
- Typically 1-2 days

**Full Course:**
- Required if expired
- Original duration
- Full cost

### Revalidation Fees

| Certificate | Revalidation Fee |
|-------------|-----------------|
| BST | ₹8,000-12,000 |
| PSCRB | ₹8,000-10,000 |
| AFF | ₹8,000-10,000 |
| MFA | ₹5,000-8,000 |

## Key Takeaways

1. **BST is foundation** - Get it during pre-sea
2. **Plan ahead** - Don't wait until expiry
3. **Budget for renewals** - Set aside ₹10K/year
4. **Choose approved institutes** - Check DG list
5. **Keep certificates safe** - Physical and digital copies
6. **Track validity** - Maintain a calendar

STCW certificates are non-negotiable. Plan, budget, and keep them current.
