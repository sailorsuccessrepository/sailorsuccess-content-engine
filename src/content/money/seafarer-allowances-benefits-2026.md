# Seafarer Allowances and Benefits 2026: Complete Guide

Beyond basic salary, seafarers receive numerous allowances and benefits that significantly increase total compensation. Understanding these components helps you evaluate job offers and ensure you receive everything you're entitled to.

## Salary Structure Overview

A typical seafarer's compensation package includes:

| Component | Percentage of Total |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Basic Salary | 50-60% |
| Fixed Overtime | 15-20% |
| Leave Pay | 10-15% |
| Other Allowances | 10-15% |
| **Total Package** | **100%** |

## Fixed Overtime (FOT)

### What is Fixed Overtime?
Instead of calculating actual overtime hours, most companies pay a fixed amount:

| Rank | Monthly FOT (₹) |
|------|----------------|
| Master | 1.5 - 2.5 lakh |
| Chief Engineer | 1.5 - 2.5 lakh |
| Chief Officer | 1.0 - 1.5 lakh |
| Second Engineer | 1.0 - 1.5 lakh |
| Second Officer | 60,000 - 90,000 |
| Third Engineer | 60,000 - 90,000 |
| Third Officer | 45,000 - 70,000 |
| Fourth Engineer | 45,000 - 70,000 |

### FOT Calculation
- Based on 103 overtime hours per month (IBF/ITF agreements)
- Hourly rate × 103 hours = Fixed Overtime

### Important Points
1. FOT is paid regardless of actual hours worked
2. Port stays may have different rates
3. Some companies pay variable overtime instead

## Leave Pay

### How Leave is Calculated
Leave accrual varies by company and agreement:

| Contract Type | Leave Earned |
|--------------|--------------|
| 4-month contract | 2 months leave |
| 6-month contract | 3 months leave |
| 8-month contract | 4 months leave |
| 9-month contract | 4.5 months leave |

### Leave Pay Calculation
- Leave pay = Basic salary for leave period
- Or consolidated as part of total package
- Paid either during leave or at sign-off

### Leave Accumulation
Some companies allow:
- Carrying forward unused leave
- Cash payment for unutilized leave
- Maximum accumulation limits apply

## Travel Allowances

### Joining Travel
**Covered by company:**
- Flight tickets to joining port
- Airport transfers
- Visa fees (if required)
- Vaccination costs

**Typical allowance:**
- International joining: ₹50,000 - 1,00,000
- Domestic joining: ₹10,000 - 30,000

### Sign-Off Travel
- Return flight tickets
- Transit accommodation
- Local transportation

### Travel Class
| Rank | Travel Class |
|------|-------------|
| Senior Officers | Business/Premium Economy |
| Junior Officers | Economy |
| Ratings | Economy |

## Bonus Payments

### Company Bonus
| Bonus Type | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Sign-on Bonus | ₹10,000 - 50,000 |
| Retention Bonus | 1-2 months salary |
| Performance Bonus | 5-10% of annual |
| Festival Bonus | ₹5,000 - 20,000 |

### Tanker/Hazard Bonus
For specialized vessels:
- Tanker allowance: 5-15% extra
- LNG/LPG premium: 10-20% extra
- Cargo bonus (some companies): Variable

## Medical Benefits

### Onboard Medical
- Free medical treatment
- Company-provided medicines
- Emergency evacuation if needed

### Medical Insurance
| Coverage | Typical Amount |
|----------|---------------|
| Hospitalization | ₹5-10 lakh |
| OPD | ₹50,000 - 1 lakh |
| Family coverage | ₹3-5 lakh |

### Illness During Leave
- Most policies cover illness within policy terms
- Pre-existing conditions may have limitations

### Injury Compensation
| Injury Type | Compensation |
|-------------|--------------|
| Partial Disability | As per CBA terms |
| Permanent Disability | 100-200 months salary |
| Death Benefit | 150-250 months salary |

## Provident Fund and Pension

### EPF for Seafarers
- Seafarers under Indian flag may have EPF
- Foreign flag - voluntary PF with manning agents

### Pension Schemes
| Scheme | Details |
|--------|---------|
| NPS | National Pension System |
| Company Pension | Some companies offer |
| Gratuity | After 5 years service |

### Gratuity Calculation
- 15 days salary × years of service ÷ 26
- Applicable after completing 5 years

## Other Allowances

### Subsistence Allowance
When signing off outside home country:
- Daily allowance for food/accommodation
- Typically $50-100 per day
- Until reaching home port

### Victualling (if applicable)
- Cash allowance in lieu of meals
- $15-25 per day (where applicable)

### Communication Allowance
| Provision | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Ship Internet | Free/subsidized |
| Phone Cards | Some companies provide |
| SIM reimbursement | ₹1,000 - 3,000 |

### Uniform Allowance
- Annual allowance: ₹5,000 - 15,000
- Or company provides uniforms directly

### Education Allowance
For upgrading certifications:
- Course fee reimbursement
- Study leave provisions
- Some companies pay full costs

## IBF/ITF Agreements

### What is IBF?
International Bargaining Forum - negotiates with ITF for:
- Minimum wages
- Overtime rates
- Leave provisions
- Working conditions

### ITF Protected Wages
| Rank | Minimum Monthly USD |
|------|-------------------|
| AB | $1,200+ |
| Third Officer | $2,800+ |
| Master | $6,500+ |

### Benefits of ITF Agreement
1. Guaranteed minimum wages
2. Standard overtime calculations
3. Leave provisions
4. Insurance requirements
5. Repatriation rights

## Tax Implications

### NRI Status Benefits
Seafarers with 184+ days outside India:
- No tax on foreign earnings
- PF contributions may have tax benefits
- NRE account benefits

### Taxable Components
Even for NRIs, some components may be taxable:
- Leave pay received in India (debatable)
- Bonuses credited to resident account
- Consult tax advisor for specifics

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is leave pay mandatory?
Yes, leave pay is a statutory requirement under most maritime regulations and CBAs.

### Can company change allowances mid-contract?
No, terms agreed at signing cannot be unilaterally changed during contract.

### What if company doesn't pay overtime?
Document actual hours worked, raise formal complaint, contact union or ITF if needed.

### Are allowances mentioned in appointment letter?
They should be. Always get complete package details in writing before signing.
