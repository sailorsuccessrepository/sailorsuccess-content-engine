# GP Rating Salary in India 2026: Complete Earnings Guide

General Purpose Rating (GP Rating) offers a solid entry into merchant navy for Class 10 pass candidates. While officer salaries grab headlines, GP ratings earn respectable incomes with lower investment and shorter training. This guide covers actual GP Rating salaries across ranks and companies in 2026.

## GP Rating Salary Structure Overview

GP Rating salaries vary based on rank, ship type, company, and flag state. Here's the complete breakdown:

### Entry Level: Trainee/Deck Cadet Rating

| Component | Monthly (USD) | Monthly (INR) |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| Basic Salary | $300-400 | ₹25,000-33,000 |
| Allowances | $50-100 | ₹4,000-8,000 |
| Overtime | $50-100 | ₹4,000-8,000 |
| **Total** | **$400-600** | **₹33,000-50,000** |

Training period typically lasts 6-12 months with stipend-level earnings.

### Ordinary Seaman (OS)

After completing GP Rating course and getting CDC, you start as OS:

| Component | Monthly (USD) | Monthly (INR) |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| Basic Salary | $600-800 | ₹50,000-66,000 |
| Allowances | $100-150 | ₹8,000-12,500 |
| Overtime | $150-250 | ₹12,500-21,000 |
| **Total** | **$850-1,200** | **₹70,500-99,600** |

OS typically serve 2-3 contracts before promotion to AB.

### Able Seaman (AB)

The standard deck rating rank with good earnings:

| Component | Monthly (USD) | Monthly (INR) |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| Basic Salary | $1,000-1,400 | ₹83,000-1,16,000 |
| Allowances | $150-200 | ₹12,500-16,600 |
| Overtime | $200-400 | ₹16,600-33,200 |
| **Total** | **$1,350-2,000** | **₹1,12,000-1,66,000** |

AB is the most common rank where GP ratings spend significant time.

### Bosun (Boatswain)

Senior-most deck rating position:

| Component | Monthly (USD) | Monthly (INR) |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| Basic Salary | $1,600-2,200 | ₹1,33,000-1,82,600 |
| Allowances | $200-300 | ₹16,600-24,900 |
| Overtime | $300-500 | ₹24,900-41,500 |
| **Total** | **$2,100-3,000** | **₹1,74,500-2,49,000** |

Bosun supervises all deck ratings and reports to Chief Officer.

## How to Maximize GP Rating Earnings

### 1. Choose the Right Ship Type

Tankers and offshore pay 20-40% more than bulk carriers. Target specialized vessels.

### 2. Work with Premium Companies

Top-tier companies like Maersk and MSC pay 15-25% more than average.

### 3. Build Specialized Skills

Additional certifications increase value:
- Crane operations
- Tank cleaning
- Advanced firefighting

### 4. Consider Longer Contracts

9-month contracts earn more annually than multiple short contracts with gaps.

### 5. Progress to Bosun

Bosun earns 50-70% more than AB. Target this promotion within 8-10 years.

## Common Questions

### Is GP Rating salary enough for good living?

Yes. AB earning ₹12-15 lakh annually can maintain comfortable lifestyle, especially in tier-2/3 cities.

### Can GP Rating become officer?

Yes, through upgrading. AB can appear for Mate (Dock) examination after sufficient sea service and study.

### Which company pays GP Rating highest?

Offshore companies and tanker operators generally pay highest. Maersk, MSC among container lines.

### How long to become Bosun?

Typically 8-12 years from GP Rating trainee to Bosun, depending on company and performance.

## Conclusion

GP Rating offers genuine career opportunity with realistic earnings of ₹8-15 lakh annually at AB rank. While not matching officer salaries, GP Rating requires minimal investment (₹1.5-2 lakh) and only 6 months training compared to 3-4 years for officers.

For Class 10 pass candidates, GP Rating provides fastest route to international merchant navy career with solid financial returns.
