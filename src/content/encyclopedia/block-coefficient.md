# Block Coefficient

## Understanding Block Coefficient

Block Coefficient is fundamental ship measurement requiring precise understanding for safe vessel operations. Indian seafarers pursuing IMU-CET entrance examinations and DG Shipping Certificate of Competency must master comprehensive knowledge of block coefficient including calculations, regulatory requirements, and practical applications.

## Technical Definition

Measure of hull fullness form efficiency

This measurement is critical for:
- Vessel stability and safety calculations
- Cargo loading and weight distribution
- Regulatory compliance with international conventions
- Port authority reporting requirements
- Commercial operations and documentation
