## LNG Operations Knowledge

### Understanding LNG Basics

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Temperature | -162°C |
| Boiling Point | -162°C at 1 atm |
| Density | ~450 kg/m³ |
| Volume Reduction | 600:1 vs gas |
| Flash Point | -188°C |

### Key Operational Concepts

**Boil-Off Gas (BOG):**
- LNG naturally evaporates
- ~0.1% of cargo daily
- Used as fuel or reliquefied
- Pressure management critical

**Custody Transfer:**
- Precise quantity measurement
- Commercial accuracy
- Ship/shore coordination
- Documentation essential

**Cool Down:**
- Gradual temperature reduction
- Prevent thermal stress
- Time-consuming process
- Critical for cargo operations

### Safety Considerations

| Hazard | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|
| Cryogenic Burns | PPE, procedures |
| Asphyxiation | Gas detection, ventilation |
| Fire | No ignition sources |
| Rollover | Cargo management |
| Rapid Phase Transition | Loading procedures |
