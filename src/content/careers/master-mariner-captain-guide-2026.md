# Master Mariner (Ship Captain): Complete Career Guide 2026

The Master Mariner, commonly known as the Ship Captain, holds the highest rank on a merchant vessel. This comprehensive guide covers everything about reaching this prestigious position.

## Who is a Master Mariner?

A Master Mariner is the commanding officer of a merchant ship, holding ultimate authority and responsibility for the vessel, crew, cargo, and voyage safety. The Master holds a Foreign Going (FG) Master Certificate of Competency issued by DG Shipping.

### Authority and Responsibility

**Absolute Authority:**
- Final decision maker on vessel
- Override any officer's decision
- Sign on/off crew members
- Legal representative of shipowner

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Safe navigation of vessel
- Cargo care and delivery
- Crew welfare and discipline
- Environmental compliance
- Commercial considerations

### Legal Status

The Master is legally responsible for:
- All aspects of vessel operation
- Statutory compliance
- Maritime law adherence
- Emergency decisions
- Environmental protection

## How to Become a Master Mariner

### Complete Career Path

**Stage 1: Pre-Sea Training (3-4 years)**
- B.Sc Nautical Science OR
- DNS after graduation

**Stage 2: Watchkeeping Officer (4-5 years)**
- Third Officer (12+ months)
- Second Officer (12+ months)
- Chief Officer Certificate

**Stage 3: Chief Officer (2-3 years)**
- Minimum 18 months as Chief Officer
- FG Master preparation

**Stage 4: Master Certificate**
- Pass FG Master examination
- Start sailing as Master

### Total Timeline: 10-14 Years

```
Pre-sea → 3/O → 2/O → C/O → Master
(3-4 yrs) (1-2 yrs) (1-2 yrs) (2-3 yrs) (Career Peak)
```

### FG Master COC Requirements

**Eligibility:**
- Valid Chief Mate (FG) Certificate
- 18 months as Chief Officer OR
- 36 months as Watchkeeping Officer (12 as C/O)

**Written Examinations:**
1. Navigation and Meteorology
2. Ship Stability and Construction
3. Cargo Handling
4. Ship Business and Law
5. Shipboard Management

**Oral Examination:**
- Emergency scenarios
- Command decisions
- International regulations
- Port State Control

## Master Mariner FAQs

### How long to become a Captain?

Minimum 10-12 years from starting pre-sea course. Typically 12-14 years.

### What's the maximum age for sailing as Master?

Most companies allow sailing until 63-65. Some until 67-68 with good health.

### Can female officers become Masters?

Absolutely. India has several successful female ship captains. The path is identical.

### Is Master COC valid worldwide?

Indian FG Master COC is recognized globally under STCW Convention.

### What if I fail FG Master oral exam?

Re-attempt after 3 months. No limit on attempts. Pass rate is around 35-40%.

### Do all Masters sail on large ships?

FG Master COC allows any size vessel. Some prefer smaller ships for better work-life balance.
