# Tanker Ship Careers 2026: Complete Guide to Oil, Chemical & Gas Carriers

Tanker ships transport the world's energy - crude oil, refined petroleum products, chemicals, and liquefied gases. For Indian seafarers, tanker careers offer premium salaries, specialized skills, and strong job security in the energy transport sector.

This comprehensive guide covers tanker types, career paths, certifications, salaries, and how to build a successful tanker career from India.

## Understanding Tanker Shipping

Tankers are specialized vessels carrying liquid cargo in bulk. Unlike container or bulk carriers, tankers require additional safety protocols, specialized training, and higher technical competence.

**Why Tanker Careers Pay More:**
- Hazardous cargo handling (flammable, toxic, explosive)
- Additional certifications required
- Strict safety standards
- Higher responsibility levels
- Specialist skills valued

**Tanker Market Size:**
- Global tanker fleet: 12,000+ vessels
- Crude oil tankers: 7,000+
- Product tankers: 3,500+
- Chemical tankers: 4,000+
- Gas carriers: 2,000+

## Required Certifications

### Basic Tanker Training (All Tanker Types)

**Oil Tanker Familiarization Course:**
- Mandatory for anyone serving on oil/chemical tankers
- Duration: 5 days
- Content: Cargo properties, safety, pollution prevention
- Cost: ₹15,000-25,000

**Chemical Tanker Familiarization Course:**
- Required for chemical tanker service
- Duration: 5 days
- Cost: ₹18,000-28,000

**Liquefied Gas Tanker Familiarization:**
- For LPG/LNG carrier service
- Duration: 5 days
- Cost: ₹20,000-30,000

### Advanced Tanker Training

Required for officers with cargo handling responsibilities.

**Advanced Oil Tanker Course:**
- For Chief Officer and above on oil tankers
- Duration: 10-12 days
- Includes: Cargo calculations, COW, IGS
- Cost: ₹40,000-60,000

**Advanced Chemical Tanker Course:**
- For senior officers on chemical tankers
- Duration: 10-12 days
- Includes: Chemical properties, compatibility, emergency response
- Cost: ₹45,000-65,000

**Advanced Gas Carrier Course:**
- For officers on LPG/LNG carriers
- Duration: 15-20 days
- Includes: Cryogenic cargo handling, reliquefaction, cargo systems
- Cost: ₹80,000-1,20,000

### IGS (Inert Gas System) Course

- Mandatory for oil tanker officers
- Duration: 3 days
- Cost: ₹12,000-18,000

### Crude Oil Washing (COW) Course

- For officers involved in crude oil cargo operations
- Duration: 3 days
- Cost: ₹12,000-18,000

## How to Enter Tanker Shipping

**For Fresh Officers:**

Step 1: Complete pre-sea training and get COC

Step 2: Join any ship type for initial experience (6-12 months minimum)

Step 3: Complete Oil/Chemical Tanker Familiarization course

Step 4: Apply to tanker companies through manning agents

Step 5: Start on product tankers (easier entry than crude/chemical)

Step 6: Progressively move to larger tankers and specialty vessels

**For Experienced Officers:**

Step 1: Complete relevant tanker familiarization course

Step 2: Get advanced tanker certification if eligible

Step 3: Apply directly to tanker companies or specialized manning agents

Step 4: Highlight any relevant liquid cargo experience

## Chemical Tanker Specialization

Chemical tankers offer highest complexity and premium salaries among tanker types.

**Why Chemical Tankers Are Special:**
- Multiple segregated tanks (20-50 tanks)
- Each tank can carry different cargo
- Complex cargo calculations
- Strict compatibility requirements
- High cleaning standards between cargoes

**Parcel Tanker Operations:**
Parcel tankers carry multiple chemical grades in single voyage. Officers must manage:
- Tank allocation
- Cargo compatibility matrices
- Tank coating protection
- Contamination prevention
- Specialized cleaning procedures

**Chemical Tanker Career Advantage:**
Chemical tanker experience opens doors to:
- Shore-based chemical logistics
- Vetting inspections
- Chemical terminal operations
- Safety consultant roles

## LNG Career Path

LNG carriers represent the premium segment of tanker shipping with highest salaries and most stringent requirements.

**Why LNG Pays Premium:**
- Cryogenic cargo (-162°C)
- Complex cargo handling systems
- High-value cargo
- Strict safety requirements
- Specialized training

**Path to LNG:**

1. Get experience on LPG carriers first (2-3 years)
2. Complete LNG familiarization course
3. Apply to LNG operators (competition is high)
4. Complete advanced gas carrier training
5. Build LNG sea time
6. Progress to senior ranks

**LNG Training Pathway:**

```
LPG Familiarization → LPG Experience (2+ years) → 
LNG Familiarization → LNG Ship Experience → 
Advanced Gas Carrier → Senior LNG Officer
```

## Tanker Company Vetting

Tanker companies are heavily vetted by oil majors (Shell, BP, ExxonMobil, etc.) who charter vessels. This means:

**For Seafarers:**
- Strict background checks
- Experience verification
- Certification verification
- Performance records reviewed
- Incident history checked

**Good Vetting Companies:**
Working for well-vetted companies enhances your CV for future tanker opportunities.

## Challenges of Tanker Work

**Operational Challenges:**
- Complex cargo operations
- Long cargo watches during loading/discharge
- Strict safety protocols
- Tank cleaning (time-consuming, physically demanding)
- Inspections by charterers (SIRE, CDI)

**Safety Concerns:**
- Flammable/explosive cargo
- Toxic gas exposure risk
- Enclosed space hazards
- Static electricity
- Environmental responsibility

**Career Challenges:**
- Higher certification costs
- Continuous training requirements
- Competition for LNG positions
- Market volatility (oil prices affect employment)
