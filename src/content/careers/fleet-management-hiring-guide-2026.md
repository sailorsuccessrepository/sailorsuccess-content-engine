# Fleet Management Ltd Hiring Guide 2026: Complete Career Information

Fleet Management Limited is one of the world's leading ship management companies, headquartered in Hong Kong. For Indian seafarers, Fleet Management offers excellent career opportunities with competitive salaries, modern vessels, and strong career development programs.

This guide covers everything you need to know about joining Fleet Management - application process, salaries, vessel types, and career growth opportunities.

## About Fleet Management Limited

Fleet Management Ltd (FML) is the ship management arm of The China Navigation Company Pte Ltd, part of the Swire Group. The company manages one of the largest and most diverse fleets globally.

**Company Profile:**

| Parameter | Details |

| Founded | 1994 |

| Global Offices | 12+ countries |
| Fleet Size | 650+ vessels |
| Seafarers Employed | 20,000+ globally |
| Parent Company | Swire Group |

**India Operations:**
- Mumbai office handles Indian seafarer recruitment
- Strong presence in Indian maritime market

- Dedicated Indian crew pool

## How to Apply

**Step 1: Online Application**

Visit Fleet Management career portal:
- Go to www.fleetship.com/careers
- Create candidate profile
- Upload documents:
  - Updated CV/Resume
  - COC and endorsements
  - STCW certificates
  - Passport copies
  - Discharge book
  - Experience letters
  - Training certificates

**Step 2: Document Verification**

Fleet Management team reviews:
- Certificate authenticity
- Experience verification
- Flag state endorsement validity
- Training compliance

**Step 3: Interview Process**

**Technical Interview:**
- Role-specific technical questions
- Practical scenarios
- Emergency procedures
- Regulatory knowledge

**HR Interview:**
- Career aspirations
- Company culture fit
- Communication assessment
- Behavioral questions

**Step 4: Medical Examination**

Complete medical by Fleet Management approved doctor:
- PEME (Pre-Employment Medical Examination)
- Drug and alcohol test
- Fitness assessment

**Step 5: Documentation & Joining**

- Sign employment contract
- Complete pre-joining formalities
- Travel arrangements by company
- Vessel assignment

## Interview Tips

**Technical Preparation:**
- Review your rank-specific competencies
- Know SOLAS, MARPOL basics
- Study vessel types FML operates
- Understand cargo operations (for tankers)
- Emergency response procedures

**Common Interview Questions:**

For Officers:
- Describe your last vessel and trade
- What were your responsibilities?
- How do you handle crew conflicts?
- Describe an emergency situation you managed
- Why do you want to join Fleet Management?

For Cadets:
- Why did you choose maritime career?
- What do you know about Fleet Management?
- What are your career goals?
- How do you handle pressure?
- Describe your training institute experience

**Presentation Tips:**
- Dress formally
- Carry all original documents
- Be punctual
- Maintain eye contact
- Speak clearly with confidence
- Be honest about experience gaps
