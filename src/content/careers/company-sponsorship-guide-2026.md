## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Missing Deadlines**: Apply well in advance  
**Poor Applications**: Incomplete or errors  
**Ignoring Medical**: Start fitness early  
**Not Researching**: Know the company  
**Overconfidence**: Prepare seriously  

## After Getting Sponsored

### During Training

- Maintain good attendance
- Score well in exams
- Participate actively
- Build relationships

### During Shipboard Training

- Follow instructions
- Learn proactively
- Complete TRB diligently
- Get good appraisals

## Conclusion

Company sponsorship is a golden opportunity. It removes the financial barrier to a maritime career. But remember:

- Competition is intense
- Preparation is crucial
- Bond is a serious commitment
- Choose company wisely
