# Cadet Sponsorship Companies in India 2026

**Company sponsorship is the golden ticket to a merchant navy career.** Instead of paying ‚Çπ15-20 lakhs for pre-sea training, companies pay for you and guarantee sea time. This guide covers the best sponsorship opportunities and how to get them.

## What is Cadet Sponsorship?

### Benefits of Sponsorship

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Training fees | Fully paid by company |
| Guaranteed sea time | 12-18 months onboard |
| Stipend during training | ‚Çπ5,000-15,000/month |
| Placement assured | Job on completion |
| Career path | Clear progression |

### The Catch: Bond Period

**Bond requirements:**
- Serve with company for 2-5 years after COC
- If you leave early, repay training costs
- Some bonds are strict, others flexible

**Worth it?** Usually YES - the training quality and guaranteed sea time outweigh the bond.

## Sponsorship Eligibility

### General Requirements

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Age | Usually 17-24 years |
| Education | 10+2 with PCM, 60%+ |
| English | 50% in English |
| Vision | As per course requirements |
| Medical | Fit for sea service |

### What Companies Look For

1. **Academic performance** - Good marks matter
2. **Communication skills** - English fluency
3. **Personality** - Leadership, teamwork
4. **Physical fitness** - Medical clearance
5. **Commitment** - Genuine interest in maritime

## Application Process

### Step 1: Research Companies

- Identify companies of interest
- Check their current intake status
- Note application deadlines
- Understand their fleet and trade

### Step 2: Prepare Documents

**Required:**
- 10th, 12th mark sheets
- Passport (if available)
- Photographs
- ID proof
- Medical fitness certificate (basic)

### Step 3: Online Application

- Fill accurately and completely
- Attach all required documents
- Write thoughtful answers to questions
- Apply to multiple companies

### Step 4: Screening Test

**Typical format:**
- Mathematics
- Physics
- English
- General aptitude
- Reasoning

**Preparation:**
- IMU CET level questions
- Focus on basics
- Practice time management

### Step 5: Interview

**Topics covered:**
- Why merchant navy?
- Why this company?
- Family support
- Career goals
- General awareness

**Tips:**
- Be honest
- Show enthusiasm
- Dress formally
- Research the company
- Ask thoughtful questions

### Step 6: Medical Examination

- Company-approved medical examiner
- Full pre-sea medical
- Vision, hearing, fitness tests
- Blood tests, X-ray
