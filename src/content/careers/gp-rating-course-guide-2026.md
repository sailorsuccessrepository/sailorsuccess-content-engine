# GP Rating Course 2026: Your Path to Merchant Navy After 10th

Think you need a degree to join Merchant Navy? Think again. GP Rating allows 10th pass candidates to start their maritime career. It's the fastest, most affordable route to the sea.

## What is GP Rating?

GP Rating (General Purpose Rating) is a 6-month pre-sea training course that prepares you for entry-level positions on ships. After completion, you can work as:

- Deck Rating (OS/AB)
- Engine Rating (Wiper/Oiler)

**Key Facts:**
- Duration: 6 months
- Eligibility: 10th pass
- Age: 17.5 to 25 years
- Entry Salary: ₹40,000 - ₹80,000/month

## Why Choose GP Rating?

### Advantages

1. **Low Education Requirement**: Only 10th pass needed
2. **Short Training**: Just 6 months
3. **Affordable**: ₹1-2 lakhs total
4. **Quick Employment**: Jobs available immediately
5. **Career Growth**: Can become officer through hawsepipe

### Who Should Consider GP Rating?

- 10th/12th pass looking for maritime career
- Those who can't afford expensive courses
- People seeking quick employment
- Those who want hands-on work

## GP Rating Course Structure

### Month 1-2: Basic Training

**Subjects:**
- Basic seamanship
- Ship construction basics
- Safety equipment
- Fire fighting basics
- Survival at sea
- First aid

### Month 3-4: Specialized Training

**Deck Stream:**
- Navigation basics
- Steering
- Cargo handling
- Rope work
- Mooring/anchoring

**Engine Stream:**
- Engine room orientation
- Hand tools
- Basic maintenance
- Machinery operation
- Workshop practice

### Month 5-6: STCW Courses

**Mandatory Courses:**
- PSSR (Personal Safety and Social Responsibility)
- PST (Personal Survival Techniques)
- EFA (Elementary First Aid)
- FPFF (Fire Prevention and Fire Fighting)

### Becoming an Officer (Hawsepipe)

GP Ratings CAN become officers:

**Deck Officer Path:**
1. Complete 36 months as AB
2. Clear competency exams
3. Get Class IV (FG) certificate
4. Join as Third Officer

**Engine Officer Path:**
1. Complete qualifying sea service
2. Pass MEO Class IV exam
3. Join as Fourth Engineer

## Common Questions

### Can I become Captain through GP Rating?
Technically possible through hawsepipe, but very rare and takes 15-20 years.

### Is GP Rating job guaranteed?
No guarantee, but placement rates are good (70-80%) from reputed institutes.

### Can girls do GP Rating?
Yes, but very few women choose ratings. More common to pursue officer courses.

### What's the contract duration?
Typically 6-9 months on ship, then 2-3 months leave.

### Is life tough as a rating?
Yes, work is physically demanding, but pay is good for 10th pass qualification.
