# Synergy Marine Jobs in India 2026: Complete Hiring Guide

Synergy Marine Group is one of the world's largest third-party ship management companies, managing over 500 vessels. With significant operations in India, Synergy offers excellent career opportunities for Indian seafarers, particularly in the tanker sector.

| Detail | Information |

| Headquarters | Singapore |
| Founded | 2006 |
| Fleet Size | 500+ vessels |
| Employees | 25,000+ (including seafarers) |

| Fleet Type | Tankers, Bulk, Container, LNG |

### Vessel Types Managed
- **Oil Tankers:** VLCCs, Suezmaxes, Aframaxes

- **Chemical Tankers:** IMO II, IMO III
- **LNG Carriers:** Modern fleet
- **Bulk Carriers:** Capesize to Handysize

## Eligibility Criteria

### For Officers
- Valid Certificate of Competency (COC)
- Minimum sea experience as per rank
- Valid STCW certificates
- Tanker endorsements (for tanker fleet)
- Company-specific courses completed
- Good English communication

### For Ratings
- Valid CDC and INDOS
- Basic STCW certificates
- Relevant experience
- Physical fitness
- No black marks in CDC

### Specific Requirements by Rank

**Third Officer/Fourth Engineer:**
- Fresh COC holders accepted
- Previous cadet training with reputed company
- Minimum 12 months cadet sea time
- Good academic record

**Second Officer/Third Engineer:**
- Minimum 12-18 months as Third Officer/Fourth Engineer
- Clean record from previous company

**Chief Officer/Second Engineer:**
- Valid Chief Mate/Second Engineer COC
- Minimum 24 months as senior officer
- Leadership capabilities demonstrated

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Synergy hire freshers?
Yes, Synergy hires fresh Third Officers and Fourth Engineers with good cadet training background.

### What is the contract duration?
Typically 6-8 months for officers, may vary based on vessel type and trade.

### Is tanker experience mandatory?
For tanker vessels, relevant endorsements are required. Experience preferred but training provided for new officers.

### How long for joining after selection?
Usually 2-4 weeks after completing documentation and courses.
