## Requirements for Cruise Ship Chef Jobs

### Basic Eligibility

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Age | 21-45 years (varies by company) |
| Education | 10th pass minimum |
| Culinary Training | Hotel Management/Chef diploma preferred |
| Experience | 1-3 years in commercial kitchen |
| English | Good communication skills |
| Physical Fitness | Able to stand 10+ hours |
| Background | Clean criminal record |

### Essential Certifications

**Maritime Certificates:**
1. **STCW Basic Safety Training**
   - PST (Personal Survival Techniques)
   - PSSR (Personal Safety & Social Responsibilities)
   - Elementary First Aid
   - Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting

2. **Medical Certificate**
   - ENG1 or equivalent
   - FDA-approved medical

3. **Additional Certificates**
   - Food Safety/HACCP
   - ServSafe (preferred)
   - Ship Security Awareness

### Culinary Qualifications

**Preferred Education:**
| Qualification | Duration | Institute Type |
|---------------|----------|----------------|
| B.Sc Hotel Management | 3-4 years | University |
| Diploma in Culinary Arts | 1-2 years | Hotel schools |
| ITI (Food Production) | 2 years | ITI |
| Short-term Chef Course | 3-6 months | Culinary institutes |

**Valued Certifications:**
- City & Guilds (UK)
- ACF Certification (USA)
- WACS Certification
- FSSAI Food Safety
