# BSM (Bernhard Schulte) Jobs in India 2026: Complete Hiring Guide

Bernhard Schulte Shipmanagement (BSM) is one of the largest independent ship managers globally, with a fleet of over 600 vessels. BSM's India operations offer substantial employment opportunities for Indian seafarers across all ranks and departments.

## About Bernhard Schulte Shipmanagement

### Company Overview
| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Headquarters | Cyprus |
| Parent Company | Schulte Group (Germany) |
| Founded | 1883 (Schulte Group) |
| Fleet Size | 600+ vessels |
| Crew Pool | 20,000+ seafarers |
| India Operations | Mumbai, Chennai |

### Vessel Types Managed
- **Container Ships:** Post-Panamax to feeder vessels
- **Tankers:** Crude, product, and chemical carriers
- **Bulk Carriers:** Capesize to Handysize
- **LNG/LPG Carriers:** Modern gas carriers
- **Offshore Vessels:** Support and supply vessels
- **Car Carriers:** PCTC vessels
- **Cruise Ships:** Various passenger vessels

## Eligibility Criteria

### General Requirements
- Valid Certificate of Competency
- STCW Basic Safety Training certificates
- Passport with 12+ months validity
- CDC with no adverse remarks
- Medical fitness certificate
- English proficiency

### Rank-Specific Requirements

**Junior Officers (Third/Fourth):**
- Valid COC
- Completed approved cadet training
- Minimum sea service as per COC
- Good academic background

**Senior Officers:**
- Relevant COC for position
- Minimum experience at lower rank
- Clean safety record
- Leadership capabilities

### Cadet Program
BSM operates a structured cadet training program:
- Partnership with approved institutes
- Sponsored training for select candidates
- Structured onboard training
- Mentorship by senior officers
