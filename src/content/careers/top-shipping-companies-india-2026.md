## How to Get Hired

### Application Process

**Step 1: Online Application**
- Create profile on company portal
- Upload certificates
- Submit application

**Step 2: Document Verification**
- All certificates verified
- Background check
- Reference check

**Step 3: Interview**
- Technical interview
- HR interview
- Medical clearance

**Step 4: Selection**
- Joining letter
- Pre-joining briefing
- Vessel assignment

### What Companies Look For

**Technical Skills:**
- Valid certificates
- Relevant experience
- Technical knowledge

**Soft Skills:**
- Communication
- Teamwork
- Leadership potential

**Documentation:**
- Complete CDC
- Valid passport
- All STCW certificates
- No gaps in documentation

### Tips for Getting Selected

1. Apply to multiple companies
2. Tailor CV for each application
3. Highlight relevant experience
4. Prepare for technical questions
5. Be honest about experience
6. Follow up professionally

## Red Flags to Avoid

### Warning Signs

- Demands money for job
- No proper office
- Unclear salary structure
- Poor online reviews
- Delayed payments history
- Old/substandard vessels
- No proper contracts

### How to Verify Companies

1. Check DG Shipping approved list
2. Read online reviews
3. Ask current employees
4. Verify office address
5. Check company registration
6. Look for news/legal issues
