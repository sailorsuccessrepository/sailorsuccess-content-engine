# Offshore Vessel Jobs for Indian Seafarers: Career Guide 2026

Offshore vessels offer Indian seafarers something rare in shipping: **equal time rotations** (28/28 or 35/35 days) with salaries matching deep-sea tankers. If work-life balance matters to you, offshore could be your ideal career path.

## Types of Offshore Vessels

### Platform Supply Vessels (PSV)

**Purpose**: Transport supplies, equipment, and provisions to offshore platforms

**Characteristics**:
- 60-100 meters length
- Large deck cargo capacity
- Specialized tanks for drilling fluids
- Dynamic positioning systems

**Operations**:
- Cargo loading/discharge operations
- Station keeping alongside platforms
- Deck cargo management
- Bulk cargo handling

### Anchor Handling Tug Supply (AHTS)

**Purpose**: Tow and anchor drilling rigs, supply operations

**Characteristics**:
- Powerful engines (10,000-25,000 BHP)
- Large winches and anchor handling equipment
- Dynamic positioning
- Fire-fighting capability

**Operations**:
- Anchor deployment and retrieval
- Rig towing operations
- Emergency response
- Platform supply

### Dive Support Vessels (DSV)

**Purpose**: Support saturation diving operations

**Characteristics**:
- Advanced DP3 positioning
- Diving systems and chambers
- ROV facilities
- Medical facilities

**Salary Premium**: 20-40% above standard OSV rates

### Accommodation/Flotel Vessels

**Purpose**: Housing offshore workers near platforms

**Large vessels**: 300-500+ personnel capacity
**Premium pay**: Hotel-style service requirements
**Stable operations**: Long-term charter positions

### Construction/Pipe-lay Vessels

**Premium specialist vessels** with highest salaries
- Pipe-laying operations
- Heavy lift capabilities
- Subsea construction
- ROV operations

## Requirements for Offshore Career

### Certificates Required

**Standard COC**
- Certificate of Competency for your rank

- Medical fitness certificate

**BOSIET (Basic Offshore Safety Induction & Emergency Training)**
- Helicopter underwater escape
- Sea survival
- Fire fighting
- First aid
- **Mandatory for all offshore workers**

**Dynamic Positioning Certification**
- DP Basic (bridge watchkeeping)
- DP Advanced (full DP operations)
- DP Unlimited (DP2/DP3 vessels)
- Essential for officer promotion

### DP Certification Path

**Step 1: DP Basic Course (5 days)**
- Theory of DP systems
- Simulator training
- Written examination
- Cost: â‚¹50,000-70,000

**Step 2: Sea Time (minimum 60 DP days)**
- Log DP hours during vessel operations
- Get logbook signed by Master/DPO
- Accumulate varied DP operations

**Step 3: DP Advanced Course (5 days)**
- Advanced theory and simulation
- Emergency procedures
- System failures

**Step 4: DP Unlimited**
- 12 months DP experience total
- Oral assessment
- Full certification

## How to Enter Offshore

### From Deep-Sea Background

**Easiest transition path:**
1. Complete BOSIET/FOET course
2. Start DP certification (DP Basic)
3. Apply to offshore companies
4. Accept initial position (even if lower rank)
5. Build offshore-specific experience
6. Progress through DP certification
7. Target premium vessels

### Direct Entry (New Officers)

Some companies offer:
- Cadet programs on offshore vessels
- Junior officer training positions
- Trainee DPO programs

### Required Documents for Application

- COC with all endorsements
- BOSIET/FOET certificate
- DP certificate (if applicable)
- Medical fitness certificate
- Passport with visa pages
- UKOA/UKOOA (for UK sector)
- Photos and CDC

## Career Progression in Offshore

**Year 1-2**: Join as 3rd Officer/4th Engineer
- Complete DP Basic

**Year 3-4**: Promote to 2nd Officer/3rd Engineer

## FAQs

### Is offshore safer than deep-sea?

Offshore has rigorous safety standards due to oil industry requirements. Fatality rates are lower than deep-sea shipping. However, operations like anchor handling have specific risks.

### Can fresh graduates join offshore?

Some companies hire cadets for offshore vessels. DP certification and BOSIET are typically required. Starting salary may be lower but progression is faster.

### What's the best region for Indian offshore officers?

Middle East offers the best combination of salary (tax-free), proximity to India, and career opportunities. Southeast Asia is also good for building experience.

### How long does DP certification take?

DP Basic: 5 days course. DP Advanced: 5 days course + 60 DP days. Full certification: 12-18 months including sea time requirements.

### Are offshore contracts permanent or contract-based?

Most are contract-based with rotational patterns. Some companies offer permanent employment with guaranteed rotations. Contract renewals depend on performance and market conditions.
