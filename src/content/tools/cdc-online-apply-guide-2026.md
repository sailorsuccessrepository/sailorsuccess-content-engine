The Continuous Discharge Certificate (CDC) is a mandatory document for every Indian seafarer. Also known as Seaman Book, it records your sea service history and is required for joining any vessel. Here's your complete guide to applying for CDC online through the DG Shipping portal in 2026.

## What is CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate)?

CDC is an official document issued by the Directorate General of Shipping, Government of India, that:

- Serves as seafarer identity document
- Records all sea service (ship name, rank, joining/leaving dates)
- Contains personal details and photograph
- Required for signing on/off any vessel
- Valid for 10 years (renewable)

**Important:** Without a valid CDC, you cannot legally work on any merchant vessel as an Indian seafarer.

## CDC Eligibility Requirements

**For New CDC Application:**

| Category | Educational Requirement | Age |
|----------|------------------------|-----|
| Officer Cadets | Approved pre-sea course admission | 17-25 years |
| Ratings | Class 10 pass + GP Rating course | 18-25 years |
| Catering | Class 10 pass + Catering course | 18-28 years |
| Electro-technical | Approved ETO course admission | 17-25 years |

**Age Relaxation:**
- 3 years for SC/ST candidates
- 5 years for ex-servicemen

## Documents Required for CDC Application

### Mandatory Documents (All Categories)

1. **INDOS Number** - Apply separately if not obtained
2. **Passport** - Valid minimum 6 months
3. **10th Class Certificate** - Date of birth proof
4. **Aadhaar Card** - Address proof
5. **Medical Fitness Certificate** - From approved doctor
6. **Passport-size Photos** - Recent, white background (4 copies)
7. **Educational Certificates** - As per category
8. **Pre-sea Training Admission Letter** - From DG approved institute

### Category-Specific Documents

**For Deck Cadets (DNS/BSc Nautical Science):**
- Institute admission letter
- IMU CET scorecard
- 12th marksheet with PCM

**For Engine Cadets (GME/Marine Engineering):**
- Institute admission letter or degree certificate
- Engineering degree (for GME)
- 12th marksheet for graduate cadets

**For GP Ratings:**
- GP Rating course admission/completion certificate
- Class 10 certificate

## Step-by-Step CDC Online Application Process

### Step 1: INDOS Registration

Before CDC application, you must have INDOS number.

**INDOS Application:**
1. Visit: https://dgshipping.gov.in
2. Go to e-Governance → Seafarer Services → INDOS
3. Fill personal details
4. Upload documents
5. Pay fee: ₹100
6. INDOS number generated instantly

### Step 2: Medical Examination

**Process:**
1. Find DG Shipping approved medical examiner
2. Complete medical examination (₹800-1500)
3. Doctor uploads certificate to DG Shipping portal
4. Wait for approval (usually 24-48 hours)

**Medical Test Components:**
- General physical examination
- Eye test (6/6 vision required, correction acceptable)
- Color blindness test
- Hearing test
- Chest X-ray
- Blood and urine tests
- ECG (for those above 40)

### Step 3: Online CDC Application

1. **Visit:** https://dgshipping.gov.in
2. **Navigate:** e-Governance → Seafarer Services → CDC
3. **Select:** New CDC Application
4. **Login:** Using INDOS credentials

**Fill Application Form:**
- Personal details (as per passport)
- Educational qualifications
- Category of CDC required
- Institute details (if applicable)
- Passport details

### Step 4: Document Upload

Upload scanned documents:
- File format: PDF or JPEG
- File size: Below 200 KB each
- Resolution: Clear and readable

**Document Checklist:**
- [ ] Passport (first and last page)
- [ ] INDOS registration
- [ ] Educational certificates
- [ ] Medical certificate (auto-fetched)
- [ ] Recent photograph
- [ ] Institute admission letter
- [ ] Address proof

### Step 5: Payment

**CDC Fee Structure:**

| Component | Fee |
|-----------|-----|
| CDC Processing | ₹500 |
| Booklet Fee | ₹200 |
| Total | ₹700 |

**Payment Methods:**
- Debit/Credit Card
- Net Banking
- UPI

### Step 6: Biometric and Photo Capture

After online application approval:

1. Receive appointment date via email/SMS
2. Visit designated Shipping Master office
3. Provide biometric (fingerprints)
4. Get photograph captured
5. Receive acknowledgment

**Shipping Master Offices:**
- Mumbai
- Chennai
- Kolkata
- Kochi
- Kandla
- Visakhapatnam
- New Delhi

### Step 7: CDC Collection

**Timeline:**
- Processing: 7-15 working days after biometric
- Collection at same Shipping Master office
- Bring original documents for verification

## CDC Renewal Process

**When to Renew:**
- Before expiry (within 6 months of expiry date)
- After 10-year validity period
- Lost or damaged CDC

**Renewal Documents:**
- Old CDC
- Valid passport
- Fresh medical certificate
- Recent photographs
- INDOS printout

**Renewal Fee:** ₹500

## Common CDC Application Problems and Solutions

### Problem 1: Medical Certificate Not Reflecting

**Solution:**
- Check if doctor uploaded to correct INDOS number
- Contact doctor to verify upload
- Wait 48 hours before raising query
- Contact DG Shipping helpdesk if persists

### Problem 2: Document Upload Errors

**Solution:**
- Ensure file size below 200 KB
- Use PDF format for multi-page documents
- Scan at 150-200 DPI
- Check file is not corrupted

### Problem 3: Payment Failed but Amount Deducted

**Solution:**
- Wait 24-48 hours for auto-refund
- Check application status
- If not resolved, email dgshipping-grievance@gov.in
- Attach payment screenshot and application number

### Problem 4: Biometric Appointment Delayed

**Solution:**
- Check spam folder for email
- Login to portal and check status
- Visit Shipping Master office for manual appointment
- Peak seasons (June-August) have longer wait times

### For Engine Cadets

- Marine Engineering degree (for degree holders)
- CDC issued as "Engine Cadet"

### For Ratings

**GP Rating CDC:**
- GP Rating course completion certificate
- CDCissued as "Trainee Rating"
- Upgradeable after sea service

**Specialized Ratings:**
- Fitter, Electrician, Pump Man
- Respective course completion required

## CDC Endorsements and Entries

**What Gets Recorded in CDC:**
- Ship name and official number
- Date of engagement and discharge
- Rank/capacity served
- Port of engagement and discharge
- Master's remarks

**Master's Endorsement:**
- "VG" (Very Good)
- "G" (Good)
- "DR" (Decline to Report)
- Negative remarks affect future employment
