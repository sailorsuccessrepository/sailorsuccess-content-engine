# CDC Application Guide 2026: Your Gateway to Sea Career

The CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate) is your official identity document as an Indian seafarer. Without it, you cannot work on any merchant vessel. This guide covers the complete application process.

## What is CDC?

**CDC = Continuous Discharge Certificate**

Also known as "Seaman's Book" or "Discharge Book", it:
- Records your sea service
- Proves your identity as a seafarer
- Contains discharge entries from vessels
- Required by all flag states
- Mandatory for employment

## Types of CDC

### Seagoing CDC
- For international voyages
- Valid for ocean-going vessels
- Most common type
- 10-year validity

### NCV CDC (Near Coastal Voyage)
- For Indian coastal waters only
- Limited to Indian flag vessels
- Shorter validity
- Simpler requirements

## Eligibility for CDC

### Basic Requirements

**Age**: Minimum 18 years

**Educational**: Varies by category
- Officers: As per pre-sea course
- Ratings: Class 10 pass (10th)

**INDOS**: Must have INDOS number

**Pre-Sea Training**: Completed approved course

**Medical**: Fit as per PEME standards

## CDC Application Process

### Step 1: Complete Prerequisites

**Before Applying**:
- INDOS registration
- Pre-sea course completion
- STCW basic courses
- Medical fitness certificate
- Police verification (some cases)

### Step 2: Online Application

**E-Samudra Portal**: esamudra.dgshipping.gov.in

1. Login with INDOS credentials
2. Select "Apply for CDC"
3. Fill application form
4. Upload documents
5. Pay fees
6. Book MMD appointment

### Step 3: Documents Upload

| Document | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| Passport photo | White background |
| Signature | Black ink on white |
| Class 10 certificate | Clear scan |
| Pre-sea course certificate | Original scan |
| STCW certificates | All basic courses |
| Medical certificate | Current validity |
| Aadhaar card | Front and back |

### Step 4: MMD Visit

**What to Bring**:
- All original documents
- Application printout
- Fees receipt
- Photos (extra copies)

**Process**:
- Document verification
- Biometric capture
- Photograph
- Signature

### Step 5: CDC Dispatch

**Timeline**: 15-30 days after MMD visit
**Delivery**: By post to registered address
**Tracking**: Available on e-Samudra

## CDC Renewal

### When to Renew

- Validity expiring within 6 months
- Pages running out
- Change in personal details
- Damaged CDC

### Renewal Process

1. Apply online via e-Samudra
2. Submit old CDC
3. Update documents if needed
4. Visit MMD for biometrics
5. Receive new CDC

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Application Rejection

**Common Reasons**:
- Poor document quality
- Information mismatch
- Incomplete documents
- Invalid medical certificate

**Solution**: Check rejection reason, rectify, reapply

### Delayed Processing

**If delayed beyond 30 days**:
- Check status on e-Samudra
- Contact MMD helpdesk
- Follow up with email

### Lost CDC

**Immediate Actions**:
1. File police report
2. Apply for duplicate via e-Samudra
3. Submit affidavit
4. Get duplicate issued

## FAQs

### How long does CDC take to arrive?

Typically 15-30 days after MMD visit. Track on e-Samudra portal.

### Can I work without CDC?

No, CDC is mandatory. You cannot join any vessel without valid CDC.

### What if my CDC is damaged?

Apply for replacement through e-Samudra. Submit damaged CDC and get new one issued.

### Is CDC valid for foreign flag ships?

Yes, Indian CDC is recognized internationally. You may need additional flag state endorsements.

### Can I apply for CDC before completing pre-sea course?

No, pre-sea course completion is mandatory before CDC application.
